Private by Architecture, Not Just Policy

CareDraft is a local-first application. The AI runs locally on your phone, so note drafting does not require an internet connection after setup, and patient data never traverses or resides on a third-party cloud.

AES-256-GCM Encryption

All on-device clinical data is encrypted at rest using high-grade keys wrapped via hardware-backed iOS Keychains or Android Keystores.

Biometric Lock & PBKDF2 PIN

Unlock the workspace with Face ID, Touch ID, or a secure PIN. Key derivation prevents brute-force attempts on local storage.

Cryptographic Recovery Code

At setup, the app derives a local recovery code. If you forget your PIN, this code allows database decryption without relying on us.

Important Limits, Plainly Stated

CareDraft is a powerful local tool, but it is built on deliberate architectural choices that dictate clear operational limitations.

AI Outputs Are Drafts

Generative speech-to-text models can mishear numbers, sides (left/right), or clinical details, or omit points. You are legally responsible for reviewing and confirming the accuracy of every word before saving to your official EHR.

Not a HIPAA Business Associate

CareDraft LLC does not store clinical information. Because we have no access to patient data, we are not a HIPAA Covered Entity or Business Associate, and we do not sign Business Associate Agreements (BAAs).

Zero Cloud Backups

There are no passwords or user accounts. All data resides strictly on your device. If you delete the app or lose your PIN + Recovery Code, your notes are permanently lost. We have no way to recover them for you.
