Washington Consumer Health Data Privacy Policy

Effective date: pending final publication. This notice supplements the general CareNote Privacy Policy.

Draft publication page. This document should be reviewed against the final app behavior and final legal identity before public launch.

1. Scope

This notice addresses Washington's My Health My Data Act. CareNote is designed as a local-first productivity app for clinicians. Clinical content entered into CareNote is intended to remain on the user's device unless the user exports it.

2. Consumer health data collected by CareNote

CareNote does not collect, receive, store, or process consumer health data on our servers. Clinical content may exist inside the app on the clinician's device, but it is not transmitted to us by the app.

3. Sources, uses, and sharing

Because CareNote does not collect consumer health data on our systems, we do not collect it from any source, use it for our own purposes, sell it, or share it with third parties.

4. User exports

If a CareNote user exports content from the app, that export is controlled by the user and handled by the destination they choose. The user is responsible for ensuring the export is permitted and appropriate.

5. Washington rights requests

Washington residents may contact privacy@carenote.app with the subject "Washington MHMDA Request." Because CareNote does not collect clinical content on our systems, our response will usually explain that any clinical content resides only on the device of the CareNote user who entered it.

6. Geofencing

CareNote does not operate a geofence around healthcare facilities and does not use geofencing technology to identify or collect information from consumers near healthcare facilities.
