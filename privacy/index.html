Privacy Policy

Effective date: pending final publication. Contact: privacy@carenote.app.

Short version: CareNote is designed so clinical content you create in the app stays on your device unless you choose to export it. We do not provide cloud note storage, user accounts, analytics tracking, advertising, or EMR integration.

1. Who publishes CareNote

CareNote is published by the final legal entity to be inserted before launch. Privacy questions can be sent to privacy@carenote.app.

2. Local clinical content

Dictation audio, transcripts, generated drafts, manually entered notes, patient information, and related clinical content are intended to remain on the device where CareNote is installed. CareNote does not transmit that clinical content to our servers and does not provide us a way to retrieve it.

This content is encrypted at rest using AES-256-GCM with a random data-encryption key that is stored only through hardware-backed key wrapping. Your PIN is not stored; it is used with PBKDF2-HMAC-SHA512 to unwrap the local encryption key. If you enable biometric unlock, the app stores a separate biometric-protected copy of that key in the iOS Keychain or Android Keystore. We do not transmit, store, back up, or have any technical means to access the encrypted content.

At setup, CareNote provides a one-time recovery code. The recovery code is the only way to regain access to your encrypted content if you forget your PIN. We do not store the recovery code; if you lose both your PIN and your recovery code, the encrypted content on your device becomes permanently inaccessible.

3. Data we may receive

The app may contact Expo Application Services for app update checks and may contact model hosting endpoints when you download on-device AI models. Those requests can include technical metadata such as IP address, platform, app version, runtime version, and device or installation identifiers needed for app functionality.

4. No analytics or advertising tracking

CareNote is built without production analytics, advertising, attribution, or crash-reporting SDKs. If this changes in a future release, the privacy policy and store privacy disclosures must be updated before that release ships.

5. Exporting content

When you copy, email, share, print, or export a note, that content leaves CareNote's encrypted local storage and is handled by the destination you choose. You are responsible for confirming that you have authority to share the content and that the destination is appropriate.

6. HIPAA and professional obligations

CareNote is not a HIPAA Covered Entity and is not a HIPAA Business Associate. We do not enter Business Associate Agreements. If your use of a tool with protected health information requires a Business Associate Agreement, CareNote is not the right tool for that use case.

7. Your choices

  • You can delete locally stored app data from within the app or by uninstalling the app.
  • You can decide whether to export content from the app.
  • You can contact us at privacy@carenote.app for privacy requests or questions.

8. Children

CareNote is not directed to children under 13 and is not intended for use by children.

9. Changes

We may update this policy when the app changes. Material privacy changes should be reflected here and in the App Store and Google Play privacy disclosures before the changed version is released.
